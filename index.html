Anthony & Amy Pujols

Why this shop exists

Anthony Pujols has fixed cars his entire career. He's a master technician, Lexus certified and ASE certified, and around here he's known for the diagnostics other shops give up on — the intermittent electrical gremlin, the check engine light that keeps coming back. He doesn't guess and he doesn't quit. When something's wrong with your car, he'll show you exactly what it is.

Amy spent years on the other side of the counter: a customer who was pressured, upsold, and talked down to by repair shops. Then she met Anthony. When they opened Oviedo Car Care in 2009, her standard became the house rule — no customer is ever pressured, judged, or talked down to. That's why our advisors don't earn commission, and why we'd rather tell you a repair can safely wait than sell you one you don't need.
